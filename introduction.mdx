## Asset classes

Trade[XYZ] covers four major asset categories, each with its own oracle design and trading hours.

### US equities

Trade perpetual futures on the stocks you know — TSLA, NVDA, AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL, and more. US equity perps reference real-time spot prices and remain tradable beyond regular trading hours (RTH), including pre-market and after-hours sessions.

### Global equities

Access perpetual futures on international names including Samsung, SK Hynix, SoftBank, and Hyundai. Global equity perps follow regional market conventions and extend your reach beyond US-listed stocks.

### Equity indices

Trade broad market exposure with perpetual futures on major global indices:

| Symbol | Underlying index |
|--------|-----------------|
| XYZ100 | Nasdaq-100 |
| SP500 | S&P 500 |
| JP225 | Nikkei 225 |
| KR200 | KOSPI 200 |
| CSI300 | CSI 300 |
| VIX | CBOE Volatility Index |

<Info>
  Index perps reference underlying futures contracts rather than spot prices. The platform rolls these contracts automatically before expiry so your exposure is always current.
</Info>

### Commodities

Gain exposure to energy, metals, and more with perpetual futures on:

- **Energy**: WTI Crude, Brent, Natural Gas
- **Precious metals**: Gold, Silver
- **Base metals**: Copper

Commodity perps track the most liquid futures contracts on CME Globex and are rolled on a regular schedule, which the platform manages for you.

### FX

Trade major foreign exchange pairs and the US Dollar Index as perpetual futures:

- EUR/USD
- USD/JPY
- DXY

FX perps run on a continuous oracle and are suited for around-the-clock currency exposure.
