## Equity indices

XYZ100, SP500, JP225, and KR200 are the four index perpetuals available on TradeXYZ. All four follow a standard quarterly roll cycle; see the [Roll Calendar](/asset-directory/roll-calendar) for exact dates.

| Symbol | Name | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **XYZ100** | XYZ100 | Tracks a modified market-cap-weighted index of 100 of the largest non-financial companies listed on a US stock exchange. Serves as a benchmark for large-cap US technology and growth equities. Keywords: `nasdaq`, `qqq` |
| **SP500** | S&P 500 | Market-cap-weighted index of 500 leading publicly traded US companies. Widely regarded as the primary gauge of large-cap US equities. Keywords: `es`, `spx` |
| **JP225** | Nikkei 225 | JPY-denominated, price-weighted index of 225 leading Japanese companies. Primary benchmark for the Japanese equity market. Keywords: `japan`, `nikkei` |
| **KR200** | KOSPI 200 | KRW-denominated, cap-weighted index of 200 large-cap South Korean companies. Primary benchmark for the South Korean equity market. Keywords: `korea`, `kospi` |
